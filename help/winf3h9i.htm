Turning Off Confirmation Messages

If you do not want a confirmation message to appear each time you delete or replace files and directories, you can change the confirmation settings. You can also turn off messages that appear when you perform certain mouse actions, such as moving and copying.

To turn off confirmation messages

1. From the Options menu, choose Confirmation.

2. In the Confirmation dialog box, clear the check boxes for the tasks you want to perform without receiving confirmation messages.

3. Choose the OK button.
