Labeling a Disk

You can assign or change a volume label for a floppy or hard disk.

To assign or change a volume label

1. If you are labeling a floppy disk, insert it in a floppy disk drive.

2. Select the drive icon for the disk you want to label.

3. From the Disk menu, choose Label Disk.

4. In the Label Disk dialog box, specify the new label.

5. Choose the OK button.
