Changing Drives

If you want to display the contents of a different disk, you can change drives in a directory window.

To change drives

Select a drive icon.

Or press TAB to select the drive bar and then the letter of the drive you want to change to.

Or from the Disk menu, choose Select Drive.

Or double-click the background of the area where the drive icons are located to open the Select Drive dialog box.
